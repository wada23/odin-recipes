Spaghetti

Description

Ingredients

  • tea or tisane
  • water
  • suger/honey/lemon/milk

Steps

  1. Take a cup or a mug.
  2. Heat some water.
  3. Place a pack of tea or tisane into your cup. Then pour the hot water in. Be very carefull.
  4. If you like, you can add some sugar, honey, lemon, milk or anything else into your cup of tea.
  5. Stir carefully, wait until the tea cools down a little and then enjoy a peacefull moment of delight.
